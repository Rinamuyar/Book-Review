Story


This platform, BridgeBurma, is a passion project created by a group of Burmese college students studying abroad who wanted to give back to their country. We thought about the best contribution we could make and locked onto a single problem: that Burmese students who went abroad lost much of their connections to the country. Our solution? To help these people stay connected, and to facilitate the sharing of their knowledge with the people back home, thus counteracting the brain drain effect. Keeping the experience and talent pool of the seniors “plugged in” will not only allow students in Myanmar to seek counsel from their seniors, but also for talents to seek out other talents from their home country on this platform.

Mission


To provide a space for sharing knowledge and cultivate collective wisdom in Myanmar.

Vision


Be the leading social enterprise in increasing talents and wisdom in Myanmar.

Values


Authenticity: Stay true to yourself and to others Nurturing: Help each other grow Compassion: Be compassionate and caring Innovation: Used the latest technology and practices to prosper Myanmar Potential: Everyone from any background can succeed Impact: Make a positive impact from little to big changes Fun: Working for BB is fun!

Slogan


Connecting Myanmar to the world.
